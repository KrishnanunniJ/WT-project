DAYS GONE COMING TO PC THIS MAY

Now that Days Gone is coming to PC, more players will be able to experience the epic journey of Deacon St. John, a former outlaw biker who is now a drifter and bounty hunter trying to survive in a post-apocalyptic open world where danger is almost everywhere. The PC version of Days Gone will feature 21:9 ultra-wide monitor support, unlocked framerates and improved graphics (increased level of details, field of view, foliage draw distances).
